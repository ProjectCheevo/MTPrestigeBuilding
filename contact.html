What to include in your enquiry

The more detail you can provide, the faster we can prepare accurate guidance. Please share:

  • Property location: Town or postcode so we can confirm travel times and logistics.
  • Project scope: Renovation goals, rooms involved, or exterior areas you want to transform.
  • Key dates: Ideal start or completion timelines, plus any planning application milestones.
  • Budget range: A working range helps us recommend the right materials and phasing.
  • Supporting material: Planning drawings, inspiration images, or existing contractor notes.

Service area & availability

We work throughout Nottinghamshire, Derbyshire, Leicestershire, Lincolnshire, and nearby counties. For projects outside these areas, please include details and we will advise on feasibility.

Our office team is available Monday to Friday, 8:00am – 6:00pm. Site visits can be arranged during evenings and Saturdays by appointment.
