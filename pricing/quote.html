Pricing & Quotations

Transparent budgets tailored to your build

Every proposal includes itemised costs, realistic timelines, and allowances for specialist trades so you can plan with confidence.

How our quoting process works

  • Discovery call: We gather goals, design inspirations, and budget priorities.
  • Site survey: Measurements, condition checks, and photographic records to inform the scope.
  • Detailed proposal: Itemised labour, materials, waste removal, and optional upgrades.
  • Programme agreement: We align on start dates, key milestones, and access requirements.

Once you approve the proposal, we issue a contract and reserve a project slot. Payment schedules are phased to match project progress.

Typical investment ranges

Costs vary depending on property size, structural requirements, and material selections. Use the guide below to frame your budget before we finalise a bespoke quote.

  • Interior refurbishments: £8,000 – £40,000 for kitchens, bathrooms, and open-plan layouts.
  • Extensions & conversions: £25,000 – £120,000 depending on footprint, utilities, and finishes.
  • Exterior works & landscaping: £5,000 – £50,000 for driveways, patios, gardens, and outdoor lighting.
  • Maintenance programmes: Tailored monthly or quarterly retainers for landlords and commercial clients.

What influences pricing?

We evaluate several factors to build accurate quotations:

  • Structural complexity: Load-bearing alterations, steelwork, and groundworks requirements.
  • Specification level: Material grade, bespoke joinery, smart technology, and sustainability goals.
  • Site logistics: Access constraints, parking, waste disposal, and protections for occupied spaces.
  • Professional services: Architectural design, planning, building control, and specialist surveys.
