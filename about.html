About MT Prestige Building

Craftsmanship, transparency, and care in every build

We are a family-run team of specialists delivering thoughtful renovations, extensions, and exterior works across the East Midlands.

Our story

MT Prestige Building began with a simple promise: to treat every property as if it were our own. From early projects restoring listed homes to large-scale refurbishments, we have always focused on detailed planning, honest communication, and finishing touches that stand the test of time.

Today, our crew brings decades of combined experience across brickwork, landscaping, roofing, and interior remodeling. Whether you are reviving a period property or modernising a commercial space, we assemble the right specialists and manage the build from first sketches to the final clean-down.

What guides our work

  • Craftsmanship first: We source proven materials, partner with trusted suppliers, and uphold rigorous site standards.
  • Clear communication: A dedicated project lead keeps you informed with schedules, progress photos, and budget checkpoints.
  • Flexible solutions: We tailor our approach to homes, rental portfolios, and commercial premises of every size.
  • Aftercare support: We remain on hand for maintenance, adjustments, and future phases once the initial works are complete.

Meet the leadership

Founder and project director Martin Taylor oversees every renovation plan, ensuring each scope aligns with client goals and local regulations. Our onsite supervisors coordinate vetted tradespeople and maintain meticulous quality checks.

We invest in ongoing training so our team can deliver energy-efficient upgrades, modern landscaping techniques, and premium finishes that increase the value of your property.

Accreditations & assurance

  • Fully insured: Public liability and employer’s liability cover for your peace of mind.
  • Trade partners: Collaboration with Gas Safe engineers, NICEIC electricians, and structural engineers as required.
  • Health & safety: Site-specific risk assessments and method statements on every project.
  • Guarantees: Workmanship warranties tailored to the scope of your build.

Your project, our partnership

We believe that the best results come from collaboration. Share your vision, inspiration boards, or planning approvals and we will craft a detailed programme that respects your budget and schedule.

Ready to explore ideas? Our team is available for site visits across Nottinghamshire, Derbyshire, Leicestershire, and the surrounding areas.
